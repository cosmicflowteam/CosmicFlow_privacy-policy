This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application SkyviewFX Weather ("App"), developed and published by CosmicFlow Team.

SkyviewFX Weather provides weather forecasts, radar imagery, home screen widgets, and optional live wallpaper features. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

SkyviewFX Weather does not require account registration and does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, mailing address, or phone number.

Depending on how you use the App, the following information may be accessed:

  • Approximate or precise device location (with your permission)
  • Weather-related preferences and saved locations
  • Diagnostic and crash information provided by Android or Google services

Location Information

Location permission is used to provide local weather forecasts, weather radar, weather alerts, widgets, and live wallpaper weather information for your current area.

You may deny location access and manually search for locations instead.

Weather Data Providers

SkyviewFX Weather obtains forecast and weather information from third-party providers, including:

  • National Weather Service (NWS) for United States weather data
  • MET Norway for international weather forecasts

Requests sent to weather providers may include geographic coordinates necessary to retrieve weather information for your selected location.

Radar Imagery

Radar imagery displayed within the App may be retrieved from publicly available weather data services. Network access is required to download radar tiles and weather updates.

Widgets and Live Wallpaper

SkyviewFX Weather may display forecast information, radar imagery, temperature, weather conditions, and selected locations through Android widgets and live wallpaper features.

These features operate locally on your device and do not publicly share your information.

In-App Purchases

SkyviewFX Weather offers optional premium features through Google Play Billing.

Purchases are securely processed by Google. CosmicFlow Team does not receive or store your payment card information.

Third-Party Services

The App may rely on Google services including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google Play Store

Information collected by Google services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy:

Google Privacy Policy

Advertising

SkyviewFX Weather currently does not display third-party advertising.

If advertising is added in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated before implementation.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Data Retention

SkyviewFX Weather does not maintain user accounts and generally does not store personal information on servers controlled by CosmicFlow Team.

Saved preferences and locations may remain stored locally on your device until removed.

Children's Privacy

SkyviewFX Weather is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • Grant or deny location access through Android settings.
  • Delete saved locations within the App.
  • Uninstall the App at any time.
