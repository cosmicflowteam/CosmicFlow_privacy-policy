This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application SkyviewFX Pro ("App"), developed and published by CosmicFlow Team.

SkyviewFX Pro provides weather forecasts, interactive weather maps, radar imagery, home screen widgets, optional live wallpaper features, and severe weather alerts. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected or accessed, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

SkyviewFX Pro does not require account registration and does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, mailing address, or phone number.

Depending on how you use the App, the following information may be accessed or stored:

  • Approximate or precise device location (only with your permission)
  • Manually searched or saved city/location names and coordinates
  • Weather-related preferences (units, theme, widget settings, map layer choices)
  • Local app settings and privacy acceptance status stored on your device
  • Diagnostic and crash information provided by Android or Google system services

CosmicFlow Team does not operate its own servers to store user accounts or personal profiles.

Location Information

Location permission is used to provide local weather forecasts, radar, map layers, weather alerts, widgets, and live wallpaper information for your selected area.

You may choose how location is used within the App, including:

  • Following your device location ("Follow me")
  • Capturing GPS once and saving a fixed location
  • Searching for and selecting a city manually

You may deny location access in Android settings and still use the App by selecting a location manually.

Weather Data Providers

SkyviewFX Pro obtains forecast and weather information from public third-party providers, including:

  • National Weather Service (NWS) — United States forecasts, observations, and alerts
  • MET Norway — international weather forecasts where NWS coverage is unavailable

Requests sent to these providers may include geographic coordinates necessary to retrieve weather information for your selected location. Those providers receive only the data needed to return weather results and are subject to their own privacy policies.

Radar, Maps, and Weather Imagery

The App displays weather maps and radar imagery using publicly available data services. Depending on your location and settings, these may include:

  • Iowa Environmental Mesonet (IEM) — U.S. NEXRAD radar mosaics
  • RainViewer — international or fallback radar composites
  • NOAA NDFD — forecast map layers such as temperature, precipitation chance, and cloud cover
  • NOAA Weather Prediction Center (WPC) — surface front analysis
  • National Hurricane Center (NHC) — tropical storm tracks, cones, and outlooks
  • CARTO — basemap tiles used behind radar and map layers

Network access is required to download weather data, radar tiles, and map imagery. Map and radar requests may include coordinates for the area you are viewing.

Widgets and Live Wallpaper

SkyviewFX Pro may display forecast information, weather conditions, metrics, radar imagery, and selected locations through Android home screen widgets and optional live wallpaper features.

Widget and wallpaper data is processed locally on your device. These features do not publicly share your information. Wallpaper and widget preferences may be stored locally on your device.

Notifications

With your permission, the App may send local notifications for severe weather alerts from the National Weather Service when enabled in settings.

You can enable or disable notifications at any time through Android system settings or within the App.

In-App Purchases and Premium Features

SkyviewFX Pro may include optional premium or enhanced features. If in-app purchases are offered in a future version, they would be processed securely by Google Play Billing. CosmicFlow Team does not receive or store your payment card information.

If no paid features are active in your version of the App, no purchase information is collected.

  • Google Play Services (including location services)
  • Google Play Store distribution and updates
  • Google Maps-related components used for map tile handling

Advertising

SkyviewFX Pro does not display third-party advertising and does not use your data for ad tracking.

If advertising is added in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated before implementation.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Data Retention

SkyviewFX Pro does not maintain user accounts and generally does not store personal information on servers controlled by CosmicFlow Team.

Saved preferences, locations, widget settings, and wallpaper choices may remain stored locally on your device until you remove them or uninstall the App. Android backup features may also store some app data according to your device settings.

Children's Privacy

SkyviewFX Pro is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • Choose manual city search instead of GPS.
  • Delete saved locations and favorites within the App.
  • Enable or disable weather alert notifications.
  • Remove widgets or disable live wallpaper at any time.
  • Uninstall the App at any time.
